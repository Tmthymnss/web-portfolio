My projects

Thrift & Stuff

This project is an online thrift store that aims to revolutionize the way people shop for second-hand items. Through our user-friendly interface and efficient search system, customers can easily browse through our curated collection, confidently knowing that each item has been carefully inspected for quality.

OLMS

This project is an innovative online library management system designed to streamline and enhance the way libraries operate. With advanced features and a user-friendly interface, our platform aims to simplify tasks such as cataloging, borrowing, returning, and tracking books. Libraries can efficiently manage their collections, update records, and handle membership details, all in one centralized location.

REDROAP

This project is an exciting online food delivery service that brings the culinary delights of numerous restaurants right to your doorstep. With just a few clicks, customers can browse through a wide variety of cuisines, select their favorite dishes, and place orders for delivery or pickup. Our platform offers a seamless and convenient experience, allowing users to customize their orders, track deliveries in real-time, and even make secure online payments.
